Junyi Shi is an independent designer currently doing her MFA at Yale School of Art. She takes graphic design as objective media to explore multi-meaning ways of communication. Using established visual symbols to challenge common experience, she reconstructs discovered things to construct undiscovered stories. Her works question the rules of normality by gatherig and refactoring them in different viewing angles.
